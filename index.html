About Me

My name is Alsoltan, and I am the creator of @realalsoltan on TikTok. My mission is simple: to spread kindness, bring people together, and make a positive impact in the lives of those who need it most.

Through my platform, I share real stories of giving—whether it’s helping Syrian refugees with yearly rent and food support, providing wheelchairs to people with disabilities, or covering surgery costs for those who cannot afford it.

On TikTok, I share videos of these efforts—not for recognition, but to inspire others to give, to care, and to believe in the power of community. I also go live daily, offering a space for entertainment, conversation, and support.

At the heart of everything I do is one belief: helping others is not just an act—it’s a way of life. My vision is to continue growing this mission, touching more lives, and building a community grounded in compassion and generosity.
